Best places to visit in Kiev

Mother Ukraine

Mother Ukraine is a monumental 62 m (203 ft) tall statue in the National Museum of the History of Ukraine in the Second World War.

The Golden Gates

The Golden Gate of Kyiv is the XXth century reconstruction of the main gate in the 11th century fortifications of Kyiv. It was named in imitation of the Golden Gate of Constantinople.

Name

Saint Sophia Cathedral is an architectural monument of Kievan Rus' built in the times of Knyaz Jaroslav the Wise. It is protected by UNESCO as the part of the World Heritage Program.
